Refund Policy

Our Refund Policy explains the conditions under which refunds, cancellations, and replacements may be provided for digital products, educational services, and future subscription-based platforms offered by DrND's Knowledge Hub™.

Effective Date: 18 July 2026 Last Updated: 18 July 2026

Introduction

DrND's Knowledge Hub™ is committed to providing high-quality educational resources and digital products. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be granted for purchases made through our website.

By purchasing any digital product or service from DrND's Knowledge Hub™, you agree to this Refund Policy.

We encourage customers to read this Refund Policy carefully before making any purchase to understand their rights and responsibilities.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refund requests will be considered only under specific circumstances described in this policy.

  • Duplicate payments.
  • Technical issues preventing access after reasonable support attempts.
  • Incorrect billing caused by our system.
  • Other exceptional situations evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
  • Refund requests must normally be submitted within 7 days of purchase unless otherwise stated.

2. Non-Refundable Items

Due to the nature of digital products, most purchases become non-refundable once the product has been downloaded, accessed, or delivered successfully.

  • Downloaded PDF books and notes.
  • Recorded video courses.
  • Digital certificates already issued.
  • Subscription periods already utilized.
  • Any product explicitly marked as non-refundable.

3. Cancellation Policy

Subscription-based services, where available, may be cancelled at any time. Cancellation will prevent future billing but does not automatically qualify for a refund of previous payments.

  • Users may cancel before the next billing cycle.
  • Access continues until the subscription expires.
  • No partial refunds for unused subscription periods unless required by law.

4. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will normally be processed through the original payment method used during purchase.

  • Processing time is generally 5–10 business days.
  • Processing time may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.
  • Confirmation will be sent by email once the refund is initiated.

5. Changes to This Refund Policy

DrND's Knowledge Hub™ reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time to reflect changes in our services, operational practices, or legal requirements.

The latest version will always be available on this page.
