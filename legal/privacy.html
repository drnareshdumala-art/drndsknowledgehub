Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how DrND's Knowledge Hub™ collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our website, educational resources, and digital services.

Effective Date: 18 July 2026 Last Updated: 18 July 2026

Introduction

DrND's Knowledge Hub™ respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our educational services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide while using DrND's Knowledge Hub™, including when you contact us, subscribe to updates, register for educational services, purchase digital products, enroll in courses, or use DrND EduAssess.

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Institution or organization (if provided)
  • Account login information
  • Payment information (processed securely through payment providers)
  • Website usage analytics and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected may be used to:

  • Provide educational resources and digital services.
  • Process purchases of digital products and services.
  • Create and manage user accounts for DrND EduAssess.
  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improve website performance and user experience.
  • Send important service updates and announcements.

3. Cookies & Analytics

DrND's Knowledge Hub™ uses cookies and similar technologies to improve website functionality, remember user preferences, analyze website traffic, and enhance the overall user experience.

We may use trusted analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to understand how visitors interact with our website. These services collect aggregated, non-personally identifiable information to help us improve our content and services.

  • Essential cookies required for website functionality.
  • Analytics cookies to understand website performance.
  • Preference cookies to remember user settings.

4. Third-Party Services

DrND's Knowledge Hub™ may integrate trusted third-party services to provide specific features and improve the overall user experience. These providers have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Examples of third-party services that may be used include:

  • Google Analytics for website traffic analysis.
  • YouTube for educational video content.
  • Razorpay or other secure payment gateways for digital product purchases.
  • Email service providers for newsletters and important service communications.
  • Cloud hosting providers for secure website operation.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of these third-party services before using their respective features.

5. Data Security & Protection

DrND's Knowledge Hub™ is committed to protecting your personal information through appropriate administrative, technical, and organizational security measures. We implement reasonable safeguards to help prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of personal data.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable security practices, no method of electronic transmission or online storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted over the internet.

  • Secure website hosting and infrastructure.
  • Restricted access to personal information.
  • Use of encrypted (HTTPS) connections where applicable.
  • Regular software updates and security maintenance.

6. Your Rights & Choices

We respect your rights regarding your personal information. Subject to applicable laws, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information that we maintain.

Depending on the services you use, you may also choose to:

  • Update or correct your account information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data where legally permitted.
  • Opt out of non-essential email communications.
  • Disable cookies through your web browser settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided at the end of this Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

DrND's Knowledge Hub™ provides educational resources intended primarily for students, educators, researchers, and professionals. Our website is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children below the age required by applicable laws without appropriate parental or guardian consent.

If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child without the necessary consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

DrND's Knowledge Hub™ may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or business practices. Any updates will be published on this page along with the revised "Last Updated" date.

We encourage users to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect personal information.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the processing of your personal information, please contact us.
